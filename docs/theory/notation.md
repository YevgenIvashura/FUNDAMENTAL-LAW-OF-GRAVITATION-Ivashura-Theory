# Notation

This document defines the key symbols used in the Ivashura Theory.

## Core quantities

- \(F(r)\) — gravitational force at distance \(r\)
- \(r\) — distance between interacting bodies
- \(\rho\) — medium density
- \(P\) — dark-energy consumption power
- \(P_1, P_2\) — dark-energy consumption power of body 1 and body 2
- \(K(\rho)\) — coefficient dependent on medium density

## Newtonian limit

- \(P = \alpha(\rho)\cdot m\)
- \(G_{\text{eff}}(\rho) = K(\rho)\cdot\alpha(\rho)^2\)
